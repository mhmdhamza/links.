Safe & Unsafe foods

Food Items Safe? Primary Nutrients Health Benefits
Carrots ✅ Vitamin A, Fiber Excellent for dental health (mechanical cleaning) and vision.
Blueberries ✅ Antioxidants, Vit C & K Supports brain health and fights cellular aging/inflammation.
Apples ✅ Fiber, Vit A & C Good for digestion. (Always remove seeds and core first)
Cooked Chicken ✅ Lean Protein Essential for muscle growth and repair. Use plain, boiled meat.
Salmon ✅ Omega-3 Fatty Acids Promotes a shiny coat, healthy skin, and reduces joint inflammation.
Pumpkin ✅ Soluble Fiber A "miracle" for digestion; helps with both diarrhea and constipation.
Green Beans ✅ Iron, Vitamins Low-calorie filler perfect for dogs needing weight management.
Cooked Eggs ✅ Protein, Riboflavin Highly digestible protein; great for an energy boost.
Chocolate ❌ Theobromine Affects the heart and nervous system; can cause seizures.
Grapes/Raisins ❌ Unknown Toxin Can lead to rapid kidney failure, even in small amounts.
Onions/Garlic ❌ Thiosulfate Damages red blood cells, leading to anemia and weakness.
Xylitol (Sweetener) ❌ Sugar Alcohol Found in "sugar-free" gum/peanut butter; causes fatal liver failure.
